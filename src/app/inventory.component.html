Our Story

We built Berecons Trading to end the premium-goods monopoly mindset.

We created Berecons Trading, LLC with one rebellious goal: end the extortionate "luxury tax" on premium goods in Ghana. We did not enter this market to build another boutique label. We entered to break a monopoly.

Our mission remains clear: authentic, mid-to-high-tier home appliances, furniture, and outdoor living essentials can be sourced, imported, and sold without predatory mark-ups.

Authentic imports Direct sourcing No extortion pricing

From Project To Purpose

What began as a private project became a mission when we saw the demand for quality. Listings for Kenmore ranges, SentrySafe, Blackstone grills, and Ozark Trail drew a strong response from buyers tired of being overcharged for products not built to last. As of February 5, only 5 units remain from our first 35 large refrigerators.

The Collection Evolves

From handcrafted Montana Woodworks pieces to refined CosmoLiving lines, we source products that reflect American and European craftsmanship. We focus on these markets for engineering quality, safety, and durability.

#TheBereconsQuality

Market Realities & Our Promise

We refuse extortion, but we also refuse shortcuts. "Cheap" often hides compromised quality or legal risk. To keep every product authentic and legally imported, we maintain a non-negotiable floor price. You pay for material quality and sourcing integrity, not hype.
