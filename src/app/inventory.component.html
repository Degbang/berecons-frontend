Our Story

Berecons Trading, LLC

Our Mission

We created Berecons Trading, LLC for a single, rebellious goal: to end the extortionate "luxury tax" on premium goods in Ghana. We entered this market to break a monopoly, not to build another "boutique" brand. Our mission remains clear: to prove that authentic, mid-to-high-tier home appliances, furniture, and outdoor living essentials can be sourced, imported, and sold without the predatory mark-ups that have become the local standard.

From Project to Purpose

What began as a private project became a mission when we saw the hunger for quality. Listings for Kenmore Ranges, SentrySafe, Blackstone Grills, and Ozark Trail received an overwhelming response from a discerning audience tired of being overcharged. for products not built to last. We read thousands of your comments, leveraged our networks to become the direct conduit for those who refuse to settle. Only 5 units of our 35 large refrigerators remain in stock since February 5th.

The Evolution of Our Collection

Our calibre continues to grow. From handcrafted, heirloom masterpieces like Montana Woodworks to the refined lines of CosmoLiving, we source pieces that define American and European craftsmanship. We specialise in these markets for their superior engineering, safety, and durability. This is the essence of #TheBereconsQuality.

Market Realities & Our Promise

We once had the "foolish" ambition to force market prices down. But importing integrity is a battle; "cheap" often masks compromised quality or legal shortcuts. To deliver authentic goods through every legal channel, we maintain a non-negotiable floor price. We refuse to participate in extortion, but we will not compromise the honesty of the journey.
